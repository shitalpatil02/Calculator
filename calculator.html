Calculator







A Simple Calculator:

This is a perfect tool for daily calculations. It has contrastive display and large colourful buttons.

  • Equals (= or Enter)
  • Clear ("C")
  • Divide (/)
  • Times (X)
  • Minus (-)
  • Plus (+)
  • Percent (% or F1) (Examples, 50%20 means 20 percent of 50; 40+50% means add 50% of 40)
